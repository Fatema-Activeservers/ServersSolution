SSL Certificate | Secure Your Data & Transactions

A digital certificate that you install on the server that hosts your website is known as an SSL certificate. SSL represents Secure Socket Layer, and it makes a protected connection between a website and a visitor's browser. Visitors to your website can have peace of mind knowing that encryption will safeguard their data and transactions and that your organization's identity has been verified when it is protected by an SSL Certificate.
